Nyamirambo

The authentic soul of Kigali — a neighbourhood alive with colour, community, and culture.

About Nyamirambo

Nyamirambo is one of the oldest and most densely populated neighbourhoods in Kigali. It sits in the Nyarugenge District and has long been the cultural heartbeat of the city. Unlike the polished downtown streets, Nyamirambo pulses with raw, authentic energy — mosques echo with the call to prayer, street vendors line every corner, and locals spill out of bars and restaurants well into the night.

It's a place where Rwandan Muslims, Christians, and people of all backgrounds have lived side-by-side for generations. Visitors come here to experience the real Kigali — not the postcard version, but the living, breathing one.

Top Things To Do

Women's Opportunity Center

A stunning piece of architecture built by Sharon Davis Design, this centre empowers local women through vocational training and craft production. Tours are available.

Culture

Evening Street Food Tour

As the sun sets, the street food scene comes alive. Brochettes on a charcoal grill, samosas, mandazi — join a guided walking tour or wander on your own.

Food

Mosque District Walk

Nyamirambo is home to several beautiful mosques including the iconic Nyamirambo Mosque with its distinctive minarets. A peaceful morning walk reveals stunning architecture.
