Top Things To Do

Must-Try Foods

  • Brochettes (grilled meat skewers)
  • Isombe (cassava leaves & eggplant)
  • Ugali with beans & plantain
  • Ikivuguto (fermented milk)
  • Mandazi (fried dough pastry)
